Compliance Statements

Compliance And Regulatory Statements

LAN ACCOUNTING FIRM as a reputable accounting firm, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance with all relevant laws and regulations, including the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA). Our team of experienced accountants and auditors ensures that our clients receive accurate and reliable financial guidance, adhering to the principles of Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and Generally Accepted Auditing Standards (GAAS).

We comply with all regulatory requirements, including those related to financial reporting, taxation, and data protection. Our firm is dedicated to providing exceptional service, maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our clients' data and complying with all relevant data regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) and the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

Assurance And Audit Compliance

Our meticulous audit process conducted in accordance with Generally Accepted Auditing Standards (GAAP) and the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants guidelines provides the assurance you need. Our experienced auditors verify the accuracy, completeness, and compliance of your financial statements. Beyond traditional audits, we offer comprehensive accounting services, including financial statement preparation, internal control evaluations, and financial reporting compliance. We proactively identify potential issues, offering solutions that streamline your financial processes and mitigate risks. Let our expertise bring clarity and confidence to your financial picture.

We adhere to all audits and accounting standards, focusing on financial reporting and internal controls. Our commitment to high-quality audits and accounting services is reflected in our unwavering professional skepticism and adherence to the principles of independence, objectivity, and integrity. Partnering with us means getting a trusted adviser dedicated to the financial health of your organization.

Tax Compliance

Tax season can be stressful. We can alleviate that burden. Our team of experienced tax professionals prepares returns in accordance with the Internal Revenue Code (IRC) and treasury regulations, maximizing your deductions and minimizing your liability. We offer comprehensive tax planning and consulting services, covering individual and business taxation, tax controversy resolutions, and tax planning for high-net-worth individuals. From tax preparation and planning to expert consulting, we handle every aspect of your tax needs, ensuring compliance and optimizing your financial position.

We comply with all tax preparation and planning standards, specializing in tax planning and consulting. Our dedication to exceptional tax services includes maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data, and complying with all relevant tax laws and regulations. With our proactive approach to tax compliance, we help you navigate the ever-changing tax landscape and achieve your financial goals.

In today's digital age, data security is paramount. We understand this.
