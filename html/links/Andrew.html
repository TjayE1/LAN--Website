Andrew Nehemia Luutu – Business Biography

Founder & CEO, LAN Accounting Firm

Master of Science in Global Health Policy and Management (STEM, Health Analytics) | CPA Candidate | Environmental Auditor | Data Analyst | Tax Professional

Andrew Nehemia Luutu is a distinguished accounting professional, data analyst, and environmental auditor with a multidisciplinary background spanning finance, tax, global health policy, and technology-driven solutions. As the Founder and CEO of LAN Accounting Firm, Andrew is dedicated to delivering cutting-edge financial, tax, and audit services, particularly for students, startups, and businesses.

Andrew holds a Master of Science in Global Health Policy and Management (STEM concentration in Health Analytics) from Brandeis University, where he honed his expertise in data science, cost-effectiveness analysis, and environmental audits. His academic background is complemented by a Bachelor’s degree in Business Computing from Makerere University Business School (MUBS), equipping him with strong analytical, programming, and financial skills.

Professional Expertise
• Accounting & Taxation: As a CPA candidate and H&R Block tax preparer, Andrew specializes in tax planning, compliance, and financial reporting. His firm, LAN Accounting Firm, offers student tax filing services, leveraging his deep connections at Brandeis University.
• Environmental Auditing & Sustainability: Andrew is an environmental auditor with a focus on sustainability and regulatory compliance. His current master’s paper on environmental audits serves as a foundational step toward securing major environmental audit projects.
• Data Analytics & Technology: With a strong command of Python, SQL, Power BI, and Stata, Andrew is well-versed in leveraging data analytics for financial and health policy decision-making. He actively incorporates data-driven insights into business and environmental auditing projects.
• Business Leadership & Consulting: Leading LAN Accounting Firm, Andrew has assembled a team of advisory board members, including CPAs, auditors, MBA students, and project managers. He also mentors and supervises students interested in environmental projects and financial analytics.

Firm & Business Initiatives
• LAN Accounting Firm (Massachusetts-based, expanding to Texas)
• Tax & accounting services for students, small businesses, and nonprofits
• Grant search & financial consulting for businesses like DW & Sons
• Data Center Initiative for health and business analytics
• Chartrflex App – an innovative rental platform

Affiliations & Certifications
• Student Member of TXCPA, AICPA, and CIA
• Mentorship under a New Jersey CPA
• EIN Registered, DBA Certificate Holder in Watertown
• Business Account with PNC, preparing for EFIN registration

With a relentless drive to innovate in accounting, environmental auditing, and data-driven financial solutions, Andrew Nehemia Luutu continues to shape the future of sustainable business and technology-driven financial services.
