Audit and Assurance Services

Audit and Assurance Services at LAN Accounting Firm are designed to provide clients with an independent and objective evaluation of their financial statements. Our audit process begins with a detailed planning phase, where we assess the client’s business environment, internal controls, and specific risks.

During the fieldwork phase, our auditors conduct a comprehensive examination of financial transactions and internal controls. This includes testing the accuracy of financial records, verifying compliance with accounting standards, and assessing the effectiveness of internal controls.

The benefits of our audit services include increased credibility with stakeholders, improved financial reporting, and the identification of operational efficiencies that can lead to cost savings.
